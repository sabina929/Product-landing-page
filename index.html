Wacom Cintiq Pro 13


Create directly on screen with our most natural pen experience
Wacom Cintiq Pro 13 is our most advanced creative pen display.
It’s built for professional artists and designers.


Three fully customizable pen buttons let you tumble objects, pan, zoom, model, sculpt or modify creative tools in 3D and 2D applications.

Wacom Color Manager is designed to give you precision color calibration, better image quality in one device calibrates everything in your workflow.

The etched-glass screen provides just the right resistance to the tip of Wacom Pro Pen 2 to give you the familiar feel of working with pen on paper.
